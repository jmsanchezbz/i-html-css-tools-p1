Queen 'The Band'

Freddie Mercury

Real Name Farrokh Bulsara
Date Of Birth Sept 5, 1946 - Nov 24, 1991
Birthplace Zanzibar, Tanzania
Instruments Vocals, piano, guitar, keyboards

The life of Farrokh Bulsara began on the East African island of Zanzibar on September 5, 1946. 25 years later in London under the name of Freddie Mercury he was fronting the now legendary rock group named Queen.

Brian May

Date Of Birth July 19, 1947>
Birthplace Twickenham, England
Instruments Vocals, piano, guitar, keyboards

With a musical career spanning four decades, Queen founding member Brian May is a world-renowned guitarist and songwriter, with production and performance credits on recordings which have sold in excess of 100 million copies worldwide.

Roger Taylor

Date Of Birth July 26, 1949
Birthplace King's Lynn, Norfolk, England
Instruments Drums, percussion, keyboards, vocals

Although best known for his powerhouse role in Queen, Roger Taylor is anything but a drummer confined to his kit. With rock n’roll in his veins all through his schooldays, he has always been a highly active, vocal member of Queen. He famously wrote Queen’s landmark hits Radio Ga Ga and A Kind Of Magic, and was also the first to make a solo album, 1981’s Fun In Space.

John Deacon

Date Of Birth August 19, 1951
Birthplace Leicester, England
Instruments Bass guitar, double bass, keyboards

John Deacon’s first guitar was a red plastic Tommy Steele special, which his parents bought for him when he was seven.
